The Bride and Groom

SARAH

Sarah dreams of a life of exploration. She has lived in both Japan and New Zealand. Sarah has an MS in Scientific and Technical Communication from the University of Minnesota – Twin Cities, and a BS in Computer Science from the University of Wisconsin – Eau Claire. She started her career doing mobile and web site development, but is currently a Technical Writer at Williams Sound.

BEN

Ben dreams of a life of adventure. He has lived in 10 different states, and also lived in New Zealand for four years. He has a BA in history from South Dakota State University. Ben started his career in software engineering coding for video games and websites, but has moved over to code deployment and server configurations. He currently is a Senior DevOps Engineer at Spok.

HOW WE MET

Ben and Sarah met while working at the Star Tribune in the Digital department. Though they knew each other as co-workers, they did not begin a relationship with one another until a few months after Sarah quit to attend graduate school at the University of Minnesota.

After Ben hosted a super bowl party that Sarah attended, Ben asked Sarah out to see Deadpool. The dates and fun nights continued for several months. Early in the relationship, Sarah moved to New Zealand for three months, a country Ben had lived in for four years during his college years. Although switching to a long distance relationship was a challenge, the new relationship lasted through and has continued to blossom ever since.

THE ENGAGEMENT

On New Years Day evening, Ben took Sarah out for a nice dinner to celebrate the new year. Since they did separate activities over New Years eve, Sarah was not at all suspicious and was very surprised when Ben proposed. She said yes, and now the delighted couple is getting married.

The Red String of Fate

THE LEGEND

The Red String of Fate is a East Asian legend often referenced in Chinese, Japanese and Korean culture. The legend states that a red string connects two people by their pinky or ankle.

The couple connected by the red string are destined to meet one another and help each other along the way. It may take each individual a while to find who they are connected to, but they are a destined couple, regardless of place, time, or circumstances. Over time, the red string may stretch or tangle, but never break.

As a couple, we vow to continue supporting each other through thick and thin. We are so happy to have finally found each other.
