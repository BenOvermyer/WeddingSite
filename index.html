HOW WE MET

Ben and Sarah met while working at the Star Tribune in the Digital department. Though they knew each other as co-workers, they did not begin a relationship with one another until a few months after Sarah quit to attend graduate school at the University of Minnesota.

After Ben hosted a Super Bowl party that Sarah attended, Ben asked her out to see Deadpool. The dates and fun nights continued for several months. Early in the relationship, Sarah moved to New Zealand for three months, a country Ben had lived in for four years during his college years. Although switching to a long distance relationship was a challenge, the new relationship lasted through and has continued to blossom ever since.

THE ENGAGEMENT

On New Year's Day evening, Ben took Sarah out for a nice dinner to celebrate the new year. Since they did separate activities over New Year's Eve, Sarah was not at all suspicious and was very surprised when Ben proposed. She said yes, and now the delighted couple is getting married.

The Red String of Fate

THE LEGEND

The Red String of Fate is a East Asian legend often referenced in Chinese, Japanese and Korean culture. The legend states that a red string connects two people by their pinky or ankle.

The couple connected by the red string are destined to meet one another and help each other along the way. It may take each individual a while to find who they are connected to, but they are a destined couple, regardless of place, time, or circumstances. Over time, the red string may stretch or tangle, but never break.

As a couple, we vow to continue supporting each other through thick and thin. We are so happy to have finally found each other.
